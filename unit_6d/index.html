ABOUT KELLY

Kelly Salamack is a creative thinker and efficient problem solver with a multidisciplinary approach to design. Kelly combines superb technological skills with a sophisticated design aesthetic. Kelly has developed the skills to translate design concepts into sought after designs because she has a strong background in consumer behavior. With a Bachelor's degree in Psychology and minor in Creative Art from Siena College, her skills allow her to create products to compliment a wide variety of specialty clients.
